Romantic Guest House

The romantic wooden guesthouse has a double bedroom with comfortable bed (1.60 x 2.00 m), a cozy living room with sofa bed, gas stove, TV, dining area, a kitchenette with fridge and Dolce gusto coffeemaker etc., a bathroom with shower and toilet, wifi and a terrace with afternoon sun. From the dining area you look out into the forest, where a group of deer regularly comes along and the badgers leave their digging tracks at night. The guesthouse is about 20 meters from our house at the edge of the forest and offers a lot of privacy. There are many cycling and walking routes, as well as restaurants and other fun destinations that we have gathered in an information folder for guests. For photos of the guesthouse see below.

Located inbetween the river De Reest and the beatiful forest's of Staphorst.

The river De Reest meanders beatiful in between Drenthe and Overijssel towards Meppel. Here, in wet times, the water can still flow outside the river banks. It is a very beautiful nature reserve that is largely managed by the Drenthe Landschap and Landschap Overijssel. Many walking and cycling routes can be found here, as well as two visitor centers of the aforementioned Landschaps. Fun activities and themed walks are regularly organized here. In the village of IJhorst and the woods around it, holidays are traditionally celebrated because of the beautiful surroundings. IJhorst has an old village center with a church near the Reest. Towards Meppel is recently a watchtower placed that offers a beautiful view of the natural winding of the Reest. Within 5 km from our B & B are five restaurants, two locations for wine tastings, a riding school, a sauna, a blueberry nursery, art exhibitions, etc. The Carstenbos (where the B & B is) is located against the large forestry village Staphorst. Here are a beautiful swimming lake, many hiking trails, a dog walk route, a children's nature experience route, beautiful fens and forest interspersed with heathland.
