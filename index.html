B&B De Bostuyn, for privacy, nature experience and pure relaxation.

We have put down an attractive wooden guest house in the garden, and you could spend the night here. On request we serve a delicious full breakfast. You can also use the wood-fired hot tub, highly recommended. Far from mass tourism in the bungalow parks, without organized "entertainment" and with the comfort you will not find on the nature campsite.

Romantic Guest House

The romantic wooden guesthouse has a double bedroom with comfortable bed (1.60 x 2.00 m), a cozy living room with sofa bed, gas stove, TV, dining area, a kitchenette with fridge and Dolce gusto coffeemaker etc., a bathroom with shower and toilet, wifi and a terrace with afternoon sun. From the dining area you look out into the forest, where a group of deer regularly comes along and the badgers leave their digging tracks at night. The guesthouse is about 20 meters from our house at the edge of the forest and offers a lot of privacy. There are many cycling and walking routes, as well as restaurants and other fun destinations that we have gathered in an information folder for guests. For photos of the guesthouse see below.
